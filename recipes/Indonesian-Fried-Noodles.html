Indonesian Fried Noodles. "Tasty and easy to make and serve"

What something different
Let's make a delicious Indonesian Fried Noodles

Ingredients

  • 8 ounces of egg noodles or ramen noodles
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • 2 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1 small onion, thinly sliced
  • 1 carrot, julienned
  • 1/2 cup cabbage, thinly sliced
  • 1/2 cup bean sprouts
  • 2 tablespoons sweet soy sauce (kecap manis)
  • 1 tablespoon soy sauce
  • 1 teaspoon oyster sauce (optional)
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground white pepper
  • Salt, to taste
  • Sliced green onions and fried shallots for garnish

Recipe instructions

  1. Cook the noodles according to the package instructions. Drain and set aside.
  2. Heat the vegetable oil in a large wok or skillet over medium-high heat.
  3. Add the minced garlic and sliced onion to the pan. Stir-fry for 2-3 minutes until fragrant and the onion becomes translucent.
  4. Add the julienned carrot, sliced cabbage, and bean sprouts to the pan. Stir-fry for another 2-3 minutes until the vegetables are slightly tender.
  5. Push the vegetables to one side of the pan, creating a space in the middle. Crack the eggs into the space and scramble them until they are cooked through.
  6. Add the cooked noodles to the pan and toss them together with the vegetables and eggs.
  7. In a small bowl, mix together the sweet soy sauce, soy sauce, oyster sauce (if using), ground white pepper, and a pinch of salt. Pour the sauce mixture over the noodles and stir-fry until the noodles are evenly coated and heated through.
  8. Taste and adjust the seasoning if needed by adding more soy sauce or salt.
  9. Remove from heat and garnish with sliced green onions and fried shallots.
  10. Serve the Indonesian fried noodles hot as a main dish. You can also serve it with a side of prawn crackers or a squeeze of lime for extra flavor.
